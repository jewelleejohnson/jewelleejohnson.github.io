Jewel Lee-Johnson

// 3D Artist — Environments, Assets & Audiovisual Worlds

Jewel Lee-Johnson is a 3D artist working at the intersection of game engines, experimental sound, and performance. Her practice centers on immersive, audio-responsive environments that blend visual systems with live interaction.

Sound is a primary driver in Jewel’s workflow. She approaches 3D space as something that can be performed and modulated in real time, allowing rhythm, frequency, and motion to shape visual form.
Drawing from DJ culture and experimental performance, her work merges procedural animation with Unreal and Unity, treating visuals as instruments rather than static assets.
Through audiovisual installations and interactive worlds, Jewel explores alternative pipelines for gaming and cinematic production—prioritizing live systems, hybridity, and underrepresented voices in 3D space.
