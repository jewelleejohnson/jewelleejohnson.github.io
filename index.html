Jewel Lee-Johnson

// 3D Artist — Environments, Assets & Audiovisual Worlds

Jewel Lee-Johnson is a 3D artist working at the intersection of game engines, experimental sound, and performance. Her practice focuses on immersive, audio-responsive environments that treat visuals as systems rather than static objects.

Sound functions as structure. Rhythm, frequency, and movement drive form, allowing environments to behave dynamically in real time rather than remaining visually fixed.

Drawing from DJ culture and experimental performance, her work merges procedural animation with Unreal and Unity, positioning visuals as instruments within live systems.
