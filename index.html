Jewel Lee-Johnson

3D artist working across game engines, experimental sound, and performance. Her practice focuses on immersive systems where visuals respond to audio, motion, and live interaction.

These environments are not static scenes, but live systems—capable of modulation, disruption, and performance.

Drawing from DJ culture and audiovisual performance, Jewel treats 3D space as an instrument—one that can be played, remixed, and reconfigured in real time.
