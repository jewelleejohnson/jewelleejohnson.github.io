Jewel Lee-Johnson

// 3D Artist — Environments, Assets & Audiovisual Worlds

Jewel Lee-Johnson is a 3D artist working at the intersection of game engines, experimental sound, and performance. Her practice centers on immersive, audio-responsive environments that treat visuals as systems rather than static objects.

Sound is a primary driver in Jewel’s workflow. She approaches 3D space as something that can be performed and modulated in real time, allowing rhythm, frequency, and movement to shape visual form.

Drawing from DJ culture and experimental performance, her work merges procedural animation with Unreal and Unity, creating environments that respond dynamically to audio and live input.
