// Environments, studies, and experimental builds

Corner

Date: Spring 2025 Role: Environment modeling, sculpting, UVs, texturing Credits: Substance Painter library for textures This environment study focuses on decayed natural structures and overgrown ruins with stonework. Inspired by my hometown in Philadelphia, barbed wire and urban decay mask the neighborhood. A sanitation workers’ strike led to trash build-up and a raw sense of collapse that would influence future projects. The goal was to push atmosphere through surface storytelling and sculpted rhythm.
