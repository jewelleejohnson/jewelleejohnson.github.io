Corner

Date: Spring 2025

Role: Environment modeling, sculpting, UVs, texturing

Credits: Substance Painter library for textures

This environment study focuses on decayed natural structures. Overgrown ruins with stonework. Inspired by my hometown in Philadelphia, with barbed wire and urban decay masking the neighborhood. There is a sanitation workers’ strike, leading to trash build-up and a raw sense of collapse that would inspire me for future projects. The goal was to push atmosphere through surface storytelling and sculpted rhythm.
